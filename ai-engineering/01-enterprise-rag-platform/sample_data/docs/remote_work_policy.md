# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

Most engineering, design, product, and corporate roles at AcmeCorp are
eligible for remote work. Specific roles that require physical presence
(lab roles, on-site customer support) are flagged in the job description.

## Work location

- Employees may work from any approved country where AcmeCorp has a legal
  entity or a registered employer-of-record (EOR) relationship.
- Tax and immigration restrictions apply. International moves require
  90 days advance notice and HR approval.
- Working from outside your home country for more than 30 days in a
  calendar year requires prior approval to manage tax exposure.

## Equipment

- AcmeCorp provides a laptop, monitor, and a one-time $500 home-office
  setup stipend.
- Employees are responsible for a reliable internet connection (minimum
  50 Mbps down / 10 Mbps up) and a quiet space for video calls.

## Core hours

Most teams maintain 4 hours of overlap per business day across team
members' time zones. Specific overlap windows are set by each team's
manager. Asynchronous-first communication is encouraged.

## Travel and on-sites

- Each team holds a quarterly in-person on-site at one of our hub offices
  (San Francisco, New York, Dublin, Singapore).
- All-company on-site once per year.
- Travel is booked through Navan; AcmeCorp covers economy airfare and a
  hotel within per diem.

## Security

- Use AcmeCorp-managed devices for all AcmeCorp work.
- Connect through the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems.
- Do not work from public networks without VPN.
- Lock your screen any time you step away.
