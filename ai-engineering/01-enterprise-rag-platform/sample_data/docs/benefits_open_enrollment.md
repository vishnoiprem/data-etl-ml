# Benefits Open Enrollment

## Annual open enrollment

The benefits open enrollment window runs from **November 1 through November 30**
each year. During this window, all eligible employees may add, remove, or
change their elections for the following plan year.

Benefits affected by open enrollment:
- Medical (PPO, HMO, HDHP)
- Dental and vision
- Health Savings Account (HSA) contributions
- Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA — healthcare and dependent care)
- Voluntary life and AD&D
- Commuter benefits

## What happens if you miss the open-enrollment window

If you do not actively elect during open enrollment, the following defaults apply:
- Your existing medical, dental, and vision elections **roll over** at the new
  premium rates.
- HSA and FSA contributions **do not** roll over and **reset to $0**. You must
  re-elect them every year.
- Voluntary life beneficiaries roll over.

After the window closes, you may only make changes if you experience a
**Qualifying Life Event (QLE)**. A QLE includes:
- Marriage, divorce, or legal separation
- Birth or adoption of a child
- Death of a spouse or dependent
- Loss or gain of other coverage (e.g., spouse's job change)
- Significant change in employment status

You have **30 days** from the date of the QLE to submit changes through the
benefits portal. After 30 days, your next opportunity to change will be the
following year's open enrollment.

## How to enroll

1. Log into BenefitsPortal at benefits.acmecorp.com.
2. Click "Open Enrollment 2026" on the dashboard.
3. Review your current elections.
4. Make changes; the system displays the new per-paycheck cost in real time.
5. Click "Submit". You will receive a confirmation email — keep it for your
   records.
