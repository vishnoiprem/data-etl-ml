# Expense and Reimbursement Policy

## Principles

- Spend AcmeCorp money as if it were your own.
- Document every expense with a receipt and a clear business purpose.
- When in doubt, ask before you spend.

## Approval thresholds

- Below $250 — manager approval, paid within 5 business days.
- $250–$2,500 — manager + Finance Business Partner approval.
- Above $2,500 — Director approval; for >$10K, VP approval.

## Common categories

| Category | Limits |
|---|---|
| Domestic flights | Economy class. Premium economy allowed for flights >5 hours. |
| International flights | Economy. Premium economy allowed >7 hours. Business class only with VP approval. |
| Hotels | Up to $300/night domestic, $400/night international. |
| Meals — solo | $75/day in cities, $50/day elsewhere. |
| Meals — team | $100/person at team dinners. |
| Ground transport | Use Lyft/Uber over taxis. Rentals only when required. |

## Submission

- Submit through Expensify within 30 days of the expense.
- Receipts are mandatory for all expenses ≥ $25.
- Personal expenses on company cards are not permitted; if unavoidable,
  flag the line item and reimburse the company.

## Non-reimbursable

- Personal entertainment (in-room movies, alcohol minibar, spa).
- Personal travel insurance beyond what AcmeCorp's travel policy covers.
- Lost personal items.
- Traffic and parking fines.

## Audit

5% of submissions are randomly audited each month. Patterns of policy
violation will be reviewed by the People Team.
